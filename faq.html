Why choose bespoke framing?

At Orlando Frames, we understand that there are some great off-the-shelf framing options if you need a quick and easy solution. However, if you’re looking for something truly unique, want to protect a special piece of artwork, or have something that’s an unusual size, bespoke framing is the perfect choice.

What frame styles do you do?

We tailor each project to suit the artwork, your taste, and your budget. Our range includes hand-finished solid wood frames, custom colors, veneers, aluminum, pre-finished black and white frames, metallics, and a variety of decorative styles. We also offer window, float, and edge-to-edge mounting. We’d be happy to discuss the best options with you when you visit!

Do you do conservation framing?

Yes, conservation framing is our standard.

Can you stretch canvas?

Absolutely!

Do you replace glass?

Yes, we do.

How long does framing take?

The timeline depends on the project, but we aim to complete most framing within three weeks of receiving the artwork. If you’re on a tight deadline, let us know.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we can deliver anywhere in the UK! Pricing depends on your location and delivery speed. If you’re too far to visit the studio, you can book an online consultation, and we’ll happily receive items by post or courier. For locals, we also offer home consultations and collection. Book your appointment here.

Do you offer ready-made frames?

We don’t carry ready-made frames in standard sizes, but we do have some exciting local collaborations that make owning bespoke framed artwork more affordable while supporting local artists and businesses. For example, check out our latest collab with the brilliant independent bookseller PKB in Walthamstow!

Who are your local partners?

Since launching Orlando Frames with a charity art auctionfor Furnishing Futures in November 2023, we’ve proudly continued our support. Furnishing Futures create healing homes for women and children who have been rehoused after escaping domestic abuse. 10% of proceedings from our frames are donated to Furnishing Futures Big Give Campaign, which is live until Dec 2024.
